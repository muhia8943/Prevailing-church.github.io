What We Stand On

Our Foundations

Four convictions shape everything Prevailing Church does — from the pulpit to the street, from Eldoret to the nations.

Our Aim

To preach the Gospel of the Kingdom of God to all Nations by word and example.

Our Mission

To know Christ and make Him known by reaching to all.

Our Vision

Touching the un-churched and the un-reached with the reached, through the Gospel, today.

Our Purpose

To bring glory to God by advancing godly life through spiritual and physical nourishment.

Who We Are

A church built to reach, not just to gather.

Prevailing Church in Eldoret is committed to advancing the Kingdom of God with urgency — standing on the Word, reaching beyond our walls, and nourishing believers in spirit and in body.

Reach

We go to the un-churched and the un-reached, carrying the gospel through the witness of those already reached.

Know

Every ministry flows from knowing Christ personally — worship, teaching, and discipleship rooted in Scripture.

Nourish

We care for the whole person, advancing godly life through both spiritual depth and practical, physical support.
