Welcome!

Welcome to Prevailing Church – Kenya

Prevailing Church – Kenya is a Christ-centered ministry committed to advancing the Kingdom of God through the preaching of the Gospel, discipleship, prayer, worship, and compassionate service. We exist to raise disciples who passionately follow Jesus Christ and impact their generation with the transforming power of His Word.

As a church, we are dedicated to building strong believers, healthy families, vibrant communities, and a lasting legacy for Christ. Through the power of the Holy Spirit, we seek to bring hope to the hopeless, strength to the weary, and salvation to all who call upon the name of the Lord.

What We Stand On

Our Foundations

Four convictions shape everything Prevailing Church does — from the pulpit to the street, from Eldoret to the nations.

Our Aim

To preach the Gospel of the Kingdom of God to all nations by word and example.

We are committed to proclaiming the message of Jesus Christ with integrity, demonstrating His love through our lives, and making His Kingdom known in every sphere of society.

Our Mission

To Know Christ and Make Him Known by Reaching All.

Our mission is to cultivate a deep and personal relationship with Jesus Christ while passionately sharing His Gospel with every person, community, and nation. We strive to raise disciples who live for Christ and lead others into a life-changing encounter with Him.

Our Vision

Touching the Un-churched and the Un-reached with the Reached through the Gospel Today.

We envision a vibrant movement of believers reaching beyond church walls to engage those who have never heard the Gospel and those who have become disconnected from the faith, bringing them into a transforming relationship with Jesus Christ.

Our Purpose

To Bring Glory to God by Advancing Godly Living through Spiritual and Physical Nourishment.

We seek to glorify God by nurturing the whole person — spiritually, emotionally, and physically — through biblical teaching, prayer, discipleship, fellowship, and practical acts of love and service.

About the Founder & Presiding Bishop

Bishop Dr. Josphat Mamet

Bishop Dr. Josphat Mamet is the Founder and Presiding Bishop of Prevailing Church – Kenya, a dynamic ministry committed to Advancing the Legacy of Christ Today through evangelism, discipleship, church planting, leadership development, and Kingdom transformation.

Called by God with a passion for the Gospel and a deep burden for souls, Bishop Mamet has dedicated his life to proclaiming the message of Jesus Christ and raising disciples who live out their faith with conviction and purpose. His ministry is driven by the zeal expressed in Matthew 11:12, believing that the advancement of God's Kingdom requires spiritual determination, bold faith, and unwavering commitment.

A visionary church planter, Bishop Mamet carries a strong apostolic burden to establish vibrant, Christ-centered congregations that serve as beacons of hope and centers of spiritual growth within their communities. His ministry reflects the heart of the Apostle Paul as expressed in 2 Corinthians 11:28, carrying a continual concern and responsibility for the well-being, growth, and strengthening of local churches.

Under his leadership, Prevailing Church – Kenya has continued to expand its reach through church planting, evangelistic missions, discipleship initiatives, leadership training, and community engagement. His vision remains focused on touching the un-churched and the un-reached with the Gospel of Jesus Christ, while equipping believers to impact their generation for God's glory.

Bishop Mamet is also an accomplished author whose writings seek to inspire spiritual growth, leadership excellence, Kingdom-minded living, and a deeper understanding of God's purpose for His people. Through his books, teachings, and mentorship, he continues to influence leaders, pastors, and believers across diverse backgrounds.

Beyond ministry, Bishop Mamet is a devoted family man who values the biblical foundation of marriage and family life. Together with his family, he exemplifies Christian faith, servant leadership, and commitment to God's calling, providing a model of godly living for both the Church and society.

As Founder and Presiding Bishop, he remains committed to building strong churches, raising faithful leaders, mentoring the next generation, and advancing God's Kingdom through the transforming power of the Gospel.
