Our Purpose

Raising Ministry-Ready Leaders

The Prevailing School of Ministry, Eldoret is a spiritual leadership institution under Prevailing Church. Its purpose is to raise biblically grounded, spiritually mature, and ministry-ready leaders who will effectively serve the church, community, and the nations.

This school is a leadership pipeline for pastors, evangelists, teachers, worship leaders, media ministers, missionaries, and marketplace reformers. As Prevailing Church continues to grow in grace, influence, and territorial impact, the need for structured ministerial training has become both urgent and prophetic.

The school is therefore a response to the divine mandate to equip faithful men and women for the work of ministry and for the expansion of Christ’s kingdom.

Vision

To raise equipped, spirit-filled, and transformational ministers for church, community, and global kingdom impact.

Mission

To provide sound biblical training, practical ministry experience, leadership development, and spiritual impartation for effective kingdom service.

Who It’s For
Pastors Evangelists Teachers Worship Leaders Media Ministers Missionaries Marketplace Reformers
Core Objectives

What The School Seeks To Do

Every objective flows from one goal: equipping faithful men and women for the work of ministry and the expansion of Christ’s kingdom.

01

Train and equip emerging ministers in biblical doctrine and ministry practice.

02

Develop church workers into effective leaders and departmental servants.

03

Prepare pastors and missionaries for church planting and frontier ministry.

04

Build strong worship, media, youth, children, and discipleship ministries.

05

Raise marketplace leaders with kingdom values and influence.

06

Preserve and transmit the spiritual DNA of Prevailing Church.
